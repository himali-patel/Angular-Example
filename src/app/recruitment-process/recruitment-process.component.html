Quick Question & Answers

How many rounds are there for an interview?

Generally we have three rounds of interviews.

1. Technical Round

2. Practical Round

3. HR Round.

In few of the cases, we opt for Online/offline Assessment Test before 1st Technical Interview.

Can we complete our both interviews (Technical & Practical) in single day?

Yes, if you can spare approx half day with us and you do not have any overlapping schedule.

How much time will it take for the technical & practical round?

Both rounds take approx. 5 to 6 hours, however it is purely based on the role and profile.

How much should I need to wait for being informed about the result of the interview ?

In most standard cases, within 3 to 5 working days, we inform you about the decision. In some special cases or situations it may take upto 8 working days to communicate final note.

How many working days are there? (5 Days or 6 Days Working)

We are following 5 Days Working Process.

[i.e: The last Saturday of the month will be working . We encourage to utilize Working Saturdays for Team Training or Fun Activities]

What do we need to bring at the time of an interview?

During interview process bring your Updated Resume & One recent Passport size Photo.[ Pleasant Smile & Self Confidence will be added as a Plus point ;) ]

Working hours - Official Time?

Our official working hours: 10:00 AM To 7:00 PM, including Lunch Break
